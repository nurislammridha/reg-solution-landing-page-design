Regulatory Compliance and Gap Analysis

Regulatory Assessment/Gap analysis of the supporting documents/data is very critical for the registration of medicinal products. read more...

Though most of the Health Authorities (HAs) accept applications/dossiers in the CTD format. Bangladesh Health Authority also accept CTD format for Import Registration but need to submit different legal documents for registration. Thus, a thorough gap analysis/technical assessment of the documents/data enables the manufacturers/importers to identify and mitigate the gaps before Regulatory submission. It will avoid Regulatory rejections of the submissions and minimize the major/critical queries to fasten the approvals. Gap analysis of the legacy dossiers against current practices followed for the manufacture of the medicinal products will ensure the Regulatory compliance of the product. The identified gaps can be mitigated by the submission of the probable supplements/variations to avoid the non-compliant Regulatory practices. REG Solution as a Regulatory service provider with the experienced Regulatory affairs resources provides gap analysis support.

Labeling Compliance

a) Pharmaceutical Product : Labeling is an interdisciplinary function that serves as the basis on which prescribing information, advertising and promotional materials for the medication are henceforth read more

created. Pharmaceutical product labeling is a complex process which involves multiple rules, regulation in Bangladesh. Proper labeling information, to effectively maintain the safety and efficacy of a drug, is essential for pharmaceutical companies.

b) BSTI Products (Food/Cosmetic/Consumer) : With modern society becoming increasingly focused on healthy eating and life style. Food/Cosmetic/Consumer products labeling helps the consumer to make an informed decision. In its simplest form, a label communicates the existing product information to the customer, thus assisting him in understanding the key ingredient, nutritional and safety information he needs to know about the product. Be it the initial product specification, the pack copy or the final artwork approval. Labeling or development of product label is a complex and challenging process. An effective and robust labeling system should ideally include a defined process, tracking tools and well-trained labeling professionals. REG Solution has experience in Core Patient Information Leaflet (Core PIL) and existing labeling setups to ensure labeling compliance as per different Bangladesh rules.

c. Literature Monitoring Services : Medical literature is an important source of information on suspected adverse reaction case reports. As regulations are becoming more stringent, marketing authorization holders (MAHs) may face the challenge of incorporating ever-increasing safety data from literature into their pharmacovigilance (PV) efforts to achieve compliance. The quality and efficiency of the literature monitoring process has a significant impact on the quality of periodic safety reports, detection of new safety signals and eventually benefit-risk evaluation (BRE) of medicinal products. REG Solution has a good Doctors team to assists organizations with specialized literature compliance services to overcome the challenge of monitoring literature.
